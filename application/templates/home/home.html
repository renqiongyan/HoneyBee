About HoneyBee Gene Database

Bee is a haploid diploid organism, its female is a diploid male is a haploid and the male does not have meiosis in the process of sperm production, so its haploid diploid has obvious sex determining mechanism.

Studying bees can help us understand many health problems, such as immune response, allergic reaction, metabolic disease, X chromosome disease, etc. The study of genetic structure diversity and gene location by using molecular biology technology can effectively study the population genetic structure of bee parasitic mite, the evolution of the genome, brain function, sexual function, immune response and disease defense mechanism, pattern differentiation, etc.
